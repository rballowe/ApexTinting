About Us

How we Got Started

Three years ago, a man had a dream to start his own buisness to tint windows. He searched for local businesses that were doing window tinting, and reached out for advice and tips. He gained some knowledge and through practice learned how to apply tint to automotive windows. Now this skill has grown into a thriving business and he has even been able to incorporate residential tinting services. It took a lot of hard work, but he was able to make his dream a reality.

Vision and Mission

Our mission is to deliver top-teir, professional automotive and residential window tinting services that elevate the aesthetics, comfort, and peace of mind our client's deserve. We envision contented customers who not only find complete satisfaction in our services but also actively advocate for our business through enthusiastic word of mouth referrals.

Customer Promise

At Apex Tinting, we take great pride in our committment to delivering the highest standard of window tinting services, and materials. Our team ensures the use of quality materials, and expert level technique. We understand that our customers needs are unique and therefore work to provide customizable solutions as well as an unparralled customer centered approach. We promise to always treat our customers with respect, provide clear and transparent pricing, and ensure customer satisfaction.
